New to cooperatives?

Your first visit can be simple.

Begin with what you are trying to achieve. The team can explain the choices and current requirements.

  1. 1

    Explain your goal

    Use ordinary words, such as “I want to save for my child.”

  2. 2

    Ask for the full picture

    Ask about documents, rates, fees, withdrawal rules and repayment dates.

  3. 3

    Decide after everything is clear

    Read the terms and keep agreements, receipts and statements.

Before you agree

Four questions every customer should ask.

You are allowed to pause, ask again and compare before deciding.

  1. 01

    What will this cost or return in total?

    Ask about rates, fees and the complete amount.

  2. 02

    When can I access the money?

    Understand withdrawal and maturity conditions.

  3. 03

    What happens if I am late?

    Ask about penalties and extra interest.

  4. 04

    Which documents should I keep?

    Keep agreements, receipts and statements.
