About Dragon Savings

A cooperative built around members, not transactions.

Dragon Savings and Credit Cooperative Limited is a primary-level, single-purpose savings and credit cooperative successfully operating since 2066 B.S. (2009).

It was established under Nepal's Cooperative Act 2048 B.S. and registered at the Division Sahakari Karyalaya, Kathmandu. The cooperative is located in Bafal-14, Kalanki, Kathmandu.
