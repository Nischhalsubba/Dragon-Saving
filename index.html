A prototype, not a bank

Your goal can be personal without becoming our data.

This version uses your browser's local storage. It does not create an account, connect to a bank, move money, or provide financial advice. Clearing browser data will remove saved progress.

Questions, answered

No smoke, mirrors, or hidden dragon clauses.

Does Dragon Saving move or store real money?

No. This is a frontend savings-tracker concept. It records the numbers you enter in your browser only and has no connection to a bank or payment service.

Where is my goal data stored?

Your goal is stored with local storage on the current browser and device. It is not synced across devices, and clearing browser data removes it.

Can I use it as financial advice?

No. Dragon Saving is a motivation and interface concept, not a financial planning, investment, or banking service.

What could a future version include?

A secure authenticated version could add multiple goals, optional cloud sync, richer progress history, reminders, and a dragon collection. Those features are not claimed in this prototype.
