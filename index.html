Common questions

Before you visit or call.

These answers help you prepare, but the cooperative should confirm current terms and requirements.

Which saving plan is best for me?

That depends on your goal, how often you can save and when you may need the money. Tell the team what you are saving for and ask them to compare suitable plans in plain language.

How do I know what a loan will really cost?

Ask for the interest rate, fees, total repayment amount, instalment schedule, late-payment consequences and any security or guarantor requirements before signing.

Can I receive remittance here?

The cooperative lists remittance services including eSewa Money Transfer, MoneyGram, Western Union and Samsara Remit. Call first to confirm the currently available provider and required identification.

Where is the office?

The office is located at Kathmandu Metropolitan City Ward 14, Bafal, Kalanki. Use the directions button or call 01-5314491 if you need help finding it.
