New to cooperatives?

Your first visit does not need to feel complicated.

Come with a goal, not a perfect understanding of finance.

  1. 1

    Explain your goal

    Use ordinary words: “I want to save for my child” or “I need money for my business.”

  2. 2

    Ask for the full picture

    Ask about documents, interest, fees, repayment dates and withdrawal rules.

  3. 3

    Take time before deciding

    Read the terms and keep copies of every agreement and receipt.
